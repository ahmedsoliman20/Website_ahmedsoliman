We got the second place in FEDA week 13 students’ National competition 2012/13 in the topic of “Bridge of the Future", competing with more than 60 other university, our project was designed to provide responsive moves that varies with the different conditions. The bridge represent the leap of faith people should take to pass from their past to the future. With a middle glass part to form an obstacle that will face people in their life, Users have to choose to step forward and take the leap to the future
